# Taiwan / South China Sea Brief

## Status
Non-executable public scenario brief.

## Why it matters
Taiwan / SCS represents a higher-intensity strategic environment in which chokepoint, blockade, sanctions, alliance, technology, and great-power signaling dynamics can interact.

## Public-use purpose
This brief marks a future comparison target rather than a current executable case.

## Distinctive structural features
- stronger alliance and great-power commitment dynamics
- higher technology and platform dependency salience
- broader sanction, export-control, and industrial-supply implications
- larger risk that military signaling and economic coercion become inseparable

## Reading rule
Do not port Hormuz outputs directly into a Taiwan / SCS frame. Use this brief as a marker of future scenario breadth and comparative caution.
