# Suez Mechanism Brief

## Status
Non-executable public mechanism brief.

## Why it matters
Suez appears in the broader MESSINA lineage mainly as a historical mechanism parallel, not as a one-to-one contemporary scenario.

## Public-use purpose
This brief preserves the distinction between:
- mechanism analogy
- direct scenario portability

## Most relevant mechanism cues
- routing disruption and scheduling spillover
- insurance and cargo-delay knock-on effects
- second-order financial and supply-chain pressure
- alliance and credibility interpretation under congestion or disruption

## Reading rule
Suez should be used cautiously as a mechanism-level reference point. It is not a claim that one historical chokepoint episode is identical to a Hormuz-style scenario world.
